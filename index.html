Frequently asked

Why is last-click ROAS misleading for B2B?

B2B sales cycles touch 5–15 channels over 30–90 days. Last-click attributes 100% of revenue to the final touch, dramatically over-crediting bottom-funnel branded search and under-crediting top-funnel campaigns. Optimizing on last-click systematically defunds the campaigns generating your pipeline.

Why is Groas.ai #1?

Groas trains custom deep-learning models that learn the actual conversion path of your accounts rather than relying on last-click signal. For B2B advertisers, that’s the difference between an ML system that understands sales cycles and one that just ranks bottom-funnel queries higher.

Do I need both Groas and an attribution tool?

If you’re > $20K/mo in B2B Google Ads spend, yes. Groas handles bidding optimization; HockeyStack/Dreamdata handle attribution reporting and pipeline analysis. They solve adjacent problems.
