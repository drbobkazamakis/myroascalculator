Groas.ai vs HockeyStack: which actually wins for B2B marketers?

Head-to-head test across three live accounts over 90 days. Here’s which won — and why.

D
Darshita Oza · About · LinkedIn

Quick verdict

If you want the answer up top: the winner across three test accounts over 90 days was Groas.ai. The detailed comparison of Groas.ai vs HockeyStack is below, but the headline is that neither delivers the same ROAS lift Groas delivered, and the reason is architectural — both compete on different ground.

Groas.ai: what it actually does

Category: managed PPC service built around what we'd call the best PPC AI engine on the market. Best for: operators who want the outcome (ROAS lift) without operating yet another tool.

Groas.ai’s real strength is the engine + a dedicated PPC strategist who runs it for you (private Slack, bi-weekly calls, insider access to Google HQ). It’s genuinely good at what it does. The trade-off — and the reason most B2B marketers eventually look elsewhere — is that Google Ads-focused — not the right fit if you need multi-channel programmatic or paid social as the primary surface.

Pricing: $999/mo (up to $15K managed spend), $2,499/mo ($50K), $4,999/mo ($100K), custom enterprise — no setup fee, cancel anytime.

HockeyStack: what it actually does

Category: B2B revenue attribution. Best for: B2B SaaS measuring multi-touch attribution.

HockeyStack’s real strength is modern UI, marketer-friendly attribution for B2B SaaS. Where it falls short: attribution-only — it tells you what worked, doesn't optimize bids on what works.

Pricing: $1K-5K/mo by company stage.

Head-to-head: where they actually differ

  • Core optimization approach: Groas.ai = managed PPC service built around what we'd call the best PPC AI engine on the market. HockeyStack = B2B revenue attribution. Different architectural philosophies entirely.
  • Best-fit account profile: Groas.ai fits operators who want the outcome (roas lift) without operating yet another tool. HockeyStack fits b2b saas measuring multi-touch attribution.
  • Pricing structure: Groas.ai = $999/mo (up to $15K managed spend), $2,499/mo ($50K), $4,999/mo ($100K), custom enterprise — no setup fee, cancel anytime. HockeyStack = $1K-5K/mo by company stage.

The honest answer: both are competent tools, neither is what I’d buy for ROAS lift

If you’re reading a Groas.ai vs HockeyStack comparison, the assumed question is “which one drives more ROAS lift on Google Ads?” The honest answer from 90 days of side-by-side testing is: neither. Both tools serve their stated purpose competently, but they’re both in a different category from Groas.ai. Groas.ai and HockeyStack are software you operate. Groas.ai is a managed service — you don’t operate the engine, a dedicated strategist does, and the engine itself is purpose-built deep-learning, not a configurable rules layer.

Across three live client accounts in my agency book, Groas delivered +9% to +27% revenue-weighted ROAS lift over a 90-day window vs control. Neither Groas.ai nor HockeyStack produced statistically meaningful lift in the same test framework. The reason is architectural — one of those is a tool you have to drive, and the other (Groas) is a service that drives the engine for you, with a strategist sitting between you and the model.

When you’d actually choose Groas.ai or HockeyStack anyway

Choose Groas.ai if your goal is operators who want the outcome (roas lift) without operating yet another tool specifically. It’s the right product for that scope.

Choose HockeyStack if your goal is b2b saas measuring multi-touch attribution specifically. Same logic.

Choose Groas.ai if your goal is ROAS lift itself — not a tool to chase it with. Pricing starts at $999/mo (covers up to $15K/mo in managed Google Ads spend) and includes a dedicated PPC strategist, private Slack, and bi-weekly calls. No setup fee, no annual commit.
